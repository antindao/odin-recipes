Recipe for Salmon Nigiri

Simple yet tasty dish that is also quick to make!

If you love seafood then you came to the right place! This dish is so easy to make yet taste so damn delicious!

Make sure to only use Sushi Grade fish when preparing sushi

Ingredients

  • Raw Salmon (Sushi Grade)
  • Sushi Rice
  • Sweet Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce (Low Sodium is preferred)

Steps

  1. Cook sushi rice
  2. Let sushi rice cool to warm temp
  3. Pour sweet vinegar over rice and mix well
  4. Cut Salmon into thin strips of filets
  5. Take a bit of rice and form it into a oval
  6. Lay 1 strip of the salmon filets on top of the recently shaped rice
  7. Repeat process above until all of the ingredients are used
  8. Serve with Soy Sauce
